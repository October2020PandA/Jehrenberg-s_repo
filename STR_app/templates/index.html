Welcome to Simple Thoughts

Simple thoughts is an interface for patients to be able to log their Simple Thought Records (STR's) anywhere, anytime and be able to share those STR's with their therapist instantly. Once an STR is shared by the patient, their therapist has the ability to read it and send any comments on each STR shared.

As a patient, your therapist needs to be registered to the platform before you can sign up and join.
