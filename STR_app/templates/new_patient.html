Welcome to Simple Thoughts

The mission of Simple Thoughts is to provide easy access to a "simple thought reccord" journal, which is used as a tool of cognative behavioral therapy. Simple thought records help to reshape thoughts that fall under a cognative distortion, allowing one to retrain their brain into more possitive experiances.
